## Full description
Wakt helps you stay connected to daily worship with accurate prayer times and a simple, respectful design. Choose automatic location or set your city manually. Get Adhan reminders, see Qibla direction, and browse the Islamic calendar.

Features:
- Precise prayer times by location
- Adhan notifications with exact alarms
- Qibla compass (device sensors)
- Islamic calendar and key dates
- Offline‑friendly font caching
- GDPR‑friendly permissions and privacy‑first design

Privacy:

- Location optional and stored on device
